WELCOME TO STUDENT MARKS CARD





INSTRUSCTIONS TO FOLLOW TO RESOLVE ERROR:


  1. To view your marks card in html: Enter the URL as :localhost:8083/Grade?Physics=(your marks)&Chemistry=(your marks)&Math=(your marks)
  2. To view your marks card in json: Enter the URL as :localhost:8083/grade-json?Physics=(your marks)&Chemistry=(your marks)&Math=(your marks)
  3. In the URL replace (your marks) with your marks obtained in the subject(eg ...physics=30&...)
  4. Enter the marks in the range of 0 to 100
  5. Dont Enter String as marks
  6. To calculate percentage use the percentage calculator on the HTML screen
  7. If you didnt attempt the paper put marks =0 (for eg ....physics=0&...)
